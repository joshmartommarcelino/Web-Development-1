Mango Graham Making

Activity | February 22, 2025

Sunshine, yellow, and sweet

our latest kitchen adventure was a tropical twist on a classic treat: Mango Graham! We layered crushed graham crackers with creamy whipped goodness and chunks of ripe, juicy mangoes, creating a dessert that was both refreshingly cool and undeniably delicious. The kids loved getting involved, smashing the graham crackers and artfully arranging the mango pieces. It was a simple, no-bake recipe that brought a burst of summer to our table, and left us all craving another slice of this vibrant, fruity delight.
